Logical Operators:
! (logical NOT)
|| (logical OR)
&& (logical AND)

Comparison Operators:
== (equal to)
!= (not equal to)
< (less than)
> (greater than)
<= (less than or equal to)
>= (greater than or equal to)

Bitwise Operators:
& (bitwise AND)
| (bitwise OR)
^ (bitwise XOR)
<< (left shift)
>> (right shift)
